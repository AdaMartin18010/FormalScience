## 1. 本体论基础

### 1.1 数学本体论

**定义 1.1.1 (数学本体论)**
数学本体论研究数学对象的存在方式和本质特征。

**柏拉图主义 (Platonism)**
数学对象客观存在于理念世界，独立于人类心智。

**形式化表达：**
$$\exists x \in \text{IdealWorld} \cdot \text{MathematicalObject}(x) \land \text{Independent}(x, \text{HumanMind})$$

**形式主义 (Formalism)**
数学是符号形式系统的操作，数学对象是符号的抽象。

**形式化表达：**
$$\text{MathematicalObject}(x) \iff \exists S \in \text{FormalSystem} \cdot x \in \text{Symbols}(S)$$

**直觉主义 (Intuitionism)**
数学是人类心智的构造，数学对象存在于构造过程中。

**形式化表达：**
$$\text{MathematicalObject}(x) \iff \exists c \in \text{Construction} \cdot x = \text{Result}(c)$$

**结构主义 (Structuralism)**
数学研究的是结构关系，数学对象是结构中的位置。

**形式化表达：**
$$\text{MathematicalObject}(x) \iff \exists S \in \text{Structure} \cdot x \in \text{Positions}(S)$$

**虚构主义 (Fictionalism)**
数学是有用的虚构，数学对象不存在但有用。

**形式化表达：**
$$\text{MathematicalObject}(x) \iff \text{Fiction}(x) \land \text{Useful}(x)$$

### 1.2 现实本体论

**定义 1.2.1 (现实本体论)**
现实本体论研究现实世界的存在方式和本质特征。

**实在论 (Realism)**
独立于心灵的客观实在存在。

**形式化表达：**
$$\exists x \cdot \text{Reality}(x) \land \text{Independent}(x, \text{Mind})$$

**反实在论 (Anti-Realism)**
依赖于心灵的实在存在。

**形式化表达：**
$$\forall x \cdot \text{Reality}(x) \rightarrow \text{Dependent}(x, \text{Mind})$$

**唯物论 (Materialism)**
物质是唯一实在。

**形式化表达：**
$$\forall x \cdot \text{Reality}(x) \rightarrow \text{Material}(x)$$

**唯心论 (Idealism)**
精神是唯一实在。

**形式化表达：**
$$\forall x \cdot \text{Reality}(x) \rightarrow \text{Mental}(x)$$

**二元论 (Dualism)**
物质和精神并立。

**形式化表达：**
$$\exists x \cdot \text{Material}(x) \land \exists y \cdot \text{Mental}(y) \land x \neq y$$

### 1.3 信息本体论

**定义 1.3.1 (信息本体论)**
信息本体论研究信息作为基础实在的理论。

**信息作为基础实在：**
$$\text{Information}(x) \rightarrow \text{FundamentalReality}(x)$$

**计算宇宙假说：**
$$\text{Universe} \equiv \text{ComputationalSystem}$$

**数字物理学：**
$$\forall p \in \text{PhysicalProcess} \cdot \exists c \in \text{Computation} \cdot p \equiv c$$

### 1.4 AI本体论

**定义 1.4.1 (AI本体论)**
AI本体论研究人工智能的存在方式和本质特征。

**强人工智能论：**
$$\text{StrongAI} \iff \text{Consciousness}(\text{AI}) \land \text{Intelligence}(\text{AI}) \geq \text{Intelligence}(\text{Human})$$

**多重实现论：**
$$\text{MultipleRealizability}(\text{Intelligence}) \iff \exists S_1, S_2 \cdot S_1 \neq S_2 \land \text{Realizes}(S_1, \text{Intelligence}) \land \text{Realizes}(S_2, \text{Intelligence})$$

**涌现主义：**
$$\text{Emergentism}(\text{Consciousness}) \iff \text{EmergesFrom}(\text{Consciousness}, \text{PhysicalProcess}) \land \text{Irreducible}(\text{Consciousness})$$

## 2. 认识论基础

### 2.1 知识论

**定义 2.1.1 (知识)**
知识是被证成的真信念。

**JTB理论 (Justified True Belief)：**
$$\text{Knowledge}(S, p) \iff \text{Believes}(S, p) \land \text{True}(p) \land \text{Justified}(S, p)$$

**葛梯尔问题 (Gettier Problem)：**
$$\exists S, p \cdot \text{Believes}(S, p) \land \text{True}(p) \land \text{Justified}(S, p) \land \neg \text{Knowledge}(S, p)$$

**知识的确证：**
$$\text{Justified}(S, p) \iff \exists e \cdot \text{Evidence}(e, p) \land \text{Has}(S, e)$$

### 2.2 真理理论

**定义 2.2.1 (真理理论)**
真理理论研究真理的本质和标准。

**符合论 (Correspondence Theory)：**
$$\text{True}(p) \iff \text{Corresponds}(p, \text{Fact})$$

**融贯论 (Coherence Theory)：**
$$\text{True}(p) \iff \text{Coheres}(p, \text{BeliefSystem})$$

**实用主义 (Pragmatism)：**
$$\text{True}(p) \iff \text{Useful}(p)$$

**紧缩论 (Deflationism)：**
$$\text{True}(p) \iff p$$

### 2.3 知识来源

**定义 2.3.1 (知识来源)**
知识来源研究知识的获得方式。

**理性主义 (Rationalism)：**
$$\text{Knowledge}(S, p) \rightarrow \text{FromReason}(S, p)$$

**经验主义 (Empiricism)：**
$$\text{Knowledge}(S, p) \rightarrow \text{FromExperience}(S, p)$$

**批判主义 (Criticism)：**
$$\text{Knowledge}(S, p) \rightarrow \text{FromCriticism}(S, p)$$

### 2.4 知识结构

**定义 2.4.1 (知识结构)**
知识结构研究知识的组织方式。

**基础主义 (Foundationalism)：**
$$\text{Knowledge}(S, p) \rightarrow \exists f \cdot \text{Foundation}(f) \land \text{Supports}(f, p)$$

**反基础主义 (Anti-Foundationalism)：**
$$\neg \exists f \cdot \text{Foundation}(f) \land \forall p \cdot \text{Knowledge}(S, p) \rightarrow \text{Circular}(p)$$

**融贯论 (Coherentism)：**
$$\text{Knowledge}(S, p) \iff \text{Coheres}(p, \text{KnowledgeSystem}(S))$$

## 3. 伦理学基础

### 3.1 规范伦理学

**定义 3.1.1 (规范伦理学)**
规范伦理学研究行为的道德标准。

**义务论 (Deontological Ethics)：**
$$\text{Right}(A) \iff \text{IntrinsicProperty}(A)$$

**功利主义 (Utilitarianism)：**
$$\text{Right}(A) \iff \text{Maximizes}(A, \text{Happiness})$$

**德性伦理学 (Virtue Ethics)：**
$$\text{Right}(A) \iff \text{Expresses}(A, \text{Virtue})$$

**关怀伦理学 (Care Ethics)：**
$$\text{Right}(A) \iff \text{Promotes}(A, \text{Care})$$

### 3.2 元伦理学

**定义 3.2.1 (元伦理学)**
元伦理学研究道德语言和道德事实的本质。

**道德实在论 (Moral Realism)：**
$$\exists f \cdot \text{MoralFact}(f) \land \text{Objective}(f)$$

**情感主义 (Emotivism)：**
$$\text{MoralJudgment}(S, p) \iff \text{EmotionalExpression}(S, p)$$

**建构主义 (Constructivism)：**
$$\text{MoralFact}(f) \iff \text{Constructed}(f, \text{Agents})$$

**错误理论 (Error Theory)：**
$$\forall p \cdot \text{MoralJudgment}(p) \rightarrow \text{False}(p)$$

### 3.3 应用伦理学

**定义 3.3.1 (应用伦理学)**
应用伦理学研究具体领域的道德问题。

**AI伦理：**
$$\text{AIEthics}(A) \iff \text{Concerns}(A, \text{AI}) \land \text{Moral}(A)$$

**工程伦理：**
$$\text{EngineeringEthics}(A) \iff \text{Concerns}(A, \text{Engineering}) \land \text{Moral}(A)$$

**科学伦理：**
$$\text{ScienceEthics}(A) \iff \text{Concerns}(A, \text{Science}) \land \text{Moral}(A)$$

**环境伦理：**
$$\text{EnvironmentalEthics}(A) \iff \text{Concerns}(A, \text{Environment}) \land \text{Moral}(A)$$

### 3.4 形式化伦理学

**定义 3.4.1 (形式化伦理学)**
形式化伦理学使用形式化方法研究伦理问题。

**道义逻辑 (Deontic Logic)：**
$$\text{Obligatory}(p) \iff \text{Required}(p)$$
$$\text{Permitted}(p) \iff \neg \text{Forbidden}(p)$$
$$\text{Forbidden}(p) \iff \text{Obligatory}(\neg p)$$

**价值对齐 (Value Alignment)：**
$$\text{Aligned}(AI, \text{HumanValues}) \iff \forall v \in \text{HumanValues} \cdot \text{Promotes}(AI, v)$$

**计算道德 (Computational Ethics)：**
$$\text{ComputationalEthics}(S) \iff \text{Implements}(S, \text{MoralReasoning})$$

## 4. 逻辑学基础

### 4.1 形式逻辑

**定义 4.1.1 (形式逻辑)**
形式逻辑研究推理的形式结构。

**命题逻辑 (Propositional Logic)：**
$$\text{Valid}(\phi \rightarrow \psi) \iff \text{Invalid}(\phi \land \neg \psi)$$

**谓词逻辑 (Predicate Logic)：**
$$\forall x \cdot P(x) \rightarrow Q(x) \iff \neg \exists x \cdot P(x) \land \neg Q(x)$$

**模态逻辑 (Modal Logic)：**
$$\Box p \iff \text{Necessary}(p)$$
$$\Diamond p \iff \text{Possible}(p)$$

**时序逻辑 (Temporal Logic)：**
$$\text{Always}(p) \iff \forall t \cdot p(t)$$
$$\text{Eventually}(p) \iff \exists t \cdot p(t)$$

### 4.2 哲学逻辑

**定义 4.2.1 (哲学逻辑)**
哲学逻辑研究哲学概念的形式化。

**认识逻辑 (Epistemic Logic)：**
$$K_i p \iff \text{Agent}_i \text{ knows } p$$
$$B_i p \iff \text{Agent}_i \text{ believes } p$$

**道义逻辑 (Deontic Logic)：**
$$O p \iff \text{Obligatory}(p)$$
$$P p \iff \text{Permitted}(p)$$

**信念逻辑 (Doxastic Logic)：**
$$B_i p \iff \text{Agent}_i \text{ believes } p$$
$$\text{BeliefRevision}(B_i, p) \rightarrow B_i'$$

**意图逻辑 (Intention Logic)：**
$$I_i p \iff \text{Agent}_i \text{ intends } p$$
$$\text{IntentionAction}(I_i, A) \rightarrow \text{Performs}(i, A)$$

### 4.3 非经典逻辑

**定义 4.3.1 (非经典逻辑)**
非经典逻辑研究经典逻辑的扩展和替代。

**直觉主义逻辑 (Intuitionistic Logic)：**
$$\neg \neg p \not\equiv p$$
$$\neg (p \lor q) \not\equiv \neg p \land \neg q$$

**模糊逻辑 (Fuzzy Logic)：**
$$\text{TruthValue}(p) \in [0, 1]$$
$$\text{AND}(a, b) = \min(a, b)$$
$$\text{OR}(a, b) = \max(a, b)$$

**非单调逻辑 (Non-Monotonic Logic)：**
$$\text{Default}(p, q) \iff \text{If } p \text{ and } \neg \text{ab } \text{ then } q$$

**多值逻辑 (Many-Valued Logic)：**
$$\text{TruthValue}(p) \in \{0, \frac{1}{2}, 1\}$$

### 4.4 逻辑哲学

**定义 4.4.1 (逻辑哲学)**
逻辑哲学研究逻辑的本质和基础。

**逻辑的本质：**
$$\text{Logic} \equiv \text{StudyOf}(\text{ValidInference})$$

**发现vs发明：**
$$\text{LogicDiscovered} \lor \text{LogicInvented}$$

**逻辑多元主义：**
$$\exists L_1, L_2 \cdot L_1 \neq L_2 \land \text{Valid}(L_1) \land \text{Valid}(L_2)$$

## 5. 形而上学基础

### 5.1 存在论

**定义 5.1.1 (存在论)**
存在论研究存在的基本范畴。

**实体 (Substance)：**
$$\text{Substance}(x) \iff \text{Independent}(x) \land \text{Substratum}(x)$$

**属性 (Property)：**
$$\text{Property}(P) \iff \exists x \cdot P(x)$$

**关系 (Relation)：**
$$\text{Relation}(R) \iff \exists x, y \cdot R(x, y)$$

**类别 (Category)：**
$$\text{Category}(C) \iff \text{Classification}(C) \land \text{Fundamental}(C)$$

### 5.2 模态形而上学

**定义 5.2.1 (模态形而上学)**
模态形而上学研究必然性和可能性。

**必然性：**
$$\Box p \iff \text{Necessary}(p) \iff \text{TrueInAllWorlds}(p)$$

**可能性：**
$$\Diamond p \iff \text{Possible}(p) \iff \text{TrueInSomeWorld}(p)$$

**可能世界：**
$$\text{PossibleWorld}(w) \iff \text{Consistent}(w) \land \text{Complete}(w)$$

**本质与偶然：**
$$\text{Essential}(P, x) \iff \forall w \cdot \text{Exists}(x, w) \rightarrow P(x, w)$$
$$\text{Accidental}(P, x) \iff \exists w \cdot \text{Exists}(x, w) \land \neg P(x, w)$$

### 5.3 时间与空间

**定义 5.3.1 (时间与空间)**
时间与空间研究时空的本质和结构。

**时间逻辑：**
$$\text{Before}(t_1, t_2) \iff t_1 < t_2$$
$$\text{After}(t_1, t_2) \iff t_2 < t_1$$
$$\text{Simultaneous}(t_1, t_2) \iff t_1 = t_2$$

**空间哲学：**
$$\text{Location}(x, l) \iff \text{At}(x, l)$$
$$\text{Distance}(l_1, l_2, d) \iff \text{Between}(l_1, l_2, d)$$

**时空关系：**
$$\text{Spacetime}(e) \iff \text{Event}(e) \land \text{Located}(e) \land \text{Timed}(e)$$

### 5.4 因果性

**定义 5.4.1 (因果性)**
因果性研究因果关系的本质。

**因果关系：**
$$\text{Causes}(c, e) \iff \text{Necessary}(c, e) \land \text{Sufficient}(c, e)$$

**决定论：**
$$\text{Determinism} \iff \forall e \cdot \exists c \cdot \text{Causes}(c, e)$$

**自由意志：**
$$\text{FreeWill}(a) \iff \text{Choice}(a) \land \neg \text{Determined}(a)$$

**概率因果：**
$$\text{ProbabilisticCauses}(c, e) \iff P(e|c) > P(e|\neg c)$$

**Haskell实现：**

```haskell
-- 哲学概念的类型定义
data Ontology = Material | Mental | Dual
data Epistemology = Rationalism | Empiricism | Criticism
data Ethics = Deontological | Utilitarian | Virtue
data Logic = Classical | Intuitionistic | Modal

-- 知识表示
type Knowledge = (Subject, Proposition, Justification)
type Subject = String
type Proposition = String
type Justification = Evidence

data Evidence = Empirical | Rational | Testimonial

-- 真理理论
class TruthTheory a where
    isTrue :: a -> Bool
    corresponds :: a -> Fact -> Bool
    coheres :: a -> [a] -> Bool

-- 道德判断
data MoralJudgment = Obligatory | Permitted | Forbidden
type Action = String
type Agent = String

-- 道义逻辑
class DeonticLogic a where
    obligatory :: a -> Bool
    permitted :: a -> Bool
    forbidden :: a -> Bool

-- 认识逻辑
class EpistemicLogic a where
    knows :: Agent -> a -> Bool
    believes :: Agent -> a -> Bool
    justified :: Agent -> a -> Bool

-- 形而上学概念
data Existence = Exists | NotExists
data Necessity = Necessary | Contingent
data Causality = Causes | NotCauses

-- 哲学推理
class PhilosophicalReasoning a where
    valid :: a -> Bool
    sound :: a -> Bool
    complete :: a -> Bool

-- 实例实现
instance TruthTheory Proposition where
    isTrue p = corresponds p (getFact p)
    corresponds p f = p == show f
    coheres p ps = all (\p' -> isConsistent p p') ps

instance DeonticLogic Action where
    obligatory a = isRequired a
    permitted a = not (forbidden a)
    forbidden a = isProhibited a

instance EpistemicLogic Proposition where
    knows agent p = hasEvidence agent p && isTrue p
    believes agent p = hasBelief agent p
    justified agent p = hasJustification agent p

-- 哲学论证
data Argument = Premise [Proposition] | Conclusion Proposition

validArgument :: Argument -> Bool
validArgument (Premise ps, Conclusion c) = 
    all isTrue ps && logicalConsequence ps c

-- 哲学系统
data PhilosophicalSystem = System {
    ontology :: Ontology,
    epistemology :: Epistemology,
    ethics :: Ethics,
    logic :: Logic
}

-- 哲学一致性检查
consistent :: PhilosophicalSystem -> Bool
consistent sys = 
    compatible (ontology sys) (epistemology sys) &&
    compatible (epistemology sys) (ethics sys) &&
    compatible (ethics sys) (logic sys)
```

    Deontological,
    Utilitarian,
    Virtue,
}

#[derive(Debug, Clone, PartialEq)]
enum Logic {
    Classical,
    Intuitionistic,
    Modal,
}

// 知识表示
#[derive(Debug, Clone)]
struct Knowledge {
    subject: String,
    proposition: String,
    justification: Evidence,
}

#[derive(Debug, Clone)]
enum Evidence {
    Empirical,
    Rational,
    Testimonial,
}

// 真理理论
trait TruthTheory {
    fn is_true(&self) -> bool;
    fn corresponds(&self, fact: &str) -> bool;
    fn coheres(&self, beliefs: &[String]) -> bool;
}

impl TruthTheory for String {
    fn is_true(&self) -> bool {
        self.corresponds(self)
    }
    
    fn corresponds(&self, fact: &str) -> bool {
        self == fact
    }
    
    fn coheres(&self, beliefs: &[String]) -> bool {
        beliefs.iter().all(|b| self != b)
    }
}

// 道德判断
#[derive(Debug, Clone, PartialEq)]
enum MoralJudgment {
    Obligatory,
    Permitted,
    Forbidden,
}

// 道义逻辑
trait DeonticLogic {
    fn obligatory(&self) -> bool;
    fn permitted(&self) -> bool;
    fn forbidden(&self) -> bool;
}

impl DeonticLogic for String {
    fn obligatory(&self) -> bool {
        self.contains("required") || self.contains("must")
    }
    
    fn permitted(&self) -> bool {
        !self.forbidden()
    }
    
    fn forbidden(&self) -> bool {
        self.contains("prohibited") || self.contains("forbidden")
    }
}

// 认识逻辑
trait EpistemicLogic {
    fn knows(&self, agent: &str, proposition: &str) -> bool;
    fn believes(&self, agent: &str, proposition: &str) -> bool;
    fn justified(&self, agent: &str, proposition: &str) -> bool;
}

struct EpistemicSystem {
    knowledge_base: HashMap<String, Vec<String>>,
    belief_base: HashMap<String, Vec<String>>,
    evidence_base: HashMap<String, Evidence>,
}

impl EpistemicLogic for EpistemicSystem {
    fn knows(&self, agent: &str, proposition: &str) -> bool {
        self.knowledge_base.get(agent)
            .map(|knowledge| knowledge.contains(&proposition.to_string()))
            .unwrap_or(false)
    }
    
    fn believes(&self, agent: &str, proposition: &str) -> bool {
        self.belief_base.get(agent)
            .map(|beliefs| beliefs.contains(&proposition.to_string()))
            .unwrap_or(false)
    }
    
    fn justified(&self, agent: &str, proposition: &str) -> bool {
        self.evidence_base.contains_key(&format!("{}:{}", agent, proposition))
    }
}

#[derive(Debug, Clone, PartialEq)]
enum Existence {
    Exists,
    NotExists,
}

#[derive(Debug, Clone, PartialEq)]
enum Necessity {
    Necessary,
    Contingent,
}

#[derive(Debug, Clone, PartialEq)]
enum Causality {
    Causes,
    NotCauses,
}

// 哲学推理
trait PhilosophicalReasoning {
    fn valid(&self) -> bool;
    fn sound(&self) -> bool;
    fn complete(&self) -> bool;
}

#[derive(Debug, Clone)]
struct Argument {
    premises: Vec<String>,
    conclusion: String,
}

impl PhilosophicalReasoning for Argument {
    fn valid(&self) -> bool {
        // 简化的有效性检查
        !self.premises.is_empty() && !self.conclusion.is_empty()
    }
    
    fn sound(&self) -> bool {
        self.valid() && self.premises.iter().all(|p| p.is_true())
    }
    
    fn complete(&self) -> bool {
        // 简化的完备性检查
        self.premises.len() >= 1
    }
}

// 哲学系统
#[derive(Debug, Clone)]
struct PhilosophicalSystem {
    ontology: Ontology,
    epistemology: Epistemology,
    ethics: Ethics,
    logic: Logic,
}

impl PhilosophicalSystem {
    fn new(ontology: Ontology, epistemology: Epistemology, ethics: Ethics, logic: Logic) -> Self {
        PhilosophicalSystem {
            ontology,
            epistemology,
            ethics,
            logic,
        }
    }
    
    fn consistent(&self) -> bool {
        // 简化的哲学一致性检查
        match (&self.ontology, &self.epistemology) {
            (Ontology::Material, Epistemology::Empiricism) => true,
            (Ontology::Mental, Epistemology::Rationalism) => true,
            (Ontology::Dual, _) => true,
            _ => false,
        }
    }
    
    fn analyze(&self) -> String {
        format!(
            "Philosophical System Analysis:\n\
             Ontology: {:?}\n\
             Epistemology: {:?}\n\
             Ethics: {:?}\n\
             Logic: {:?}\n\
             Consistent: {}",
            self.ontology,
            self.epistemology,
            self.ethics,
            self.logic,
            self.consistent()
        )
    }
}

## 8. 批判分析

### 8.1 哲学批判

**定义 8.1.1 (哲学批判)**
哲学批判是对哲学理论和论证的批判性分析。

**逻辑批判：**
$$\text{LogicalCriticism}(T) \iff \text{Invalid}(T) \lor \text{Inconsistent}(T)$$

**概念批判：**
$$\text{ConceptualCriticism}(T) \iff \text{Unclear}(T) \lor \text{Ambiguous}(T)$$

**经验批判：**
$$\text{EmpiricalCriticism}(T) \iff \text{Contradicts}(T, \text{Experience})$$

### 8.2 科学批判

**定义 8.2.1 (科学批判)**
科学批判是对科学理论和方法的批判性分析。

**方法论批判：**
$$\text{MethodologicalCriticism}(S) \iff \text{FlawedMethod}(S)$$

**证据批判：**
$$\text{EvidenceCriticism}(S) \iff \text{InsufficientEvidence}(S)$$

**理论批判：**
$$\text{TheoreticalCriticism}(S) \iff \text{Inconsistent}(S) \lor \text{Incomplete}(S)$$

### 8.3 跨学科批判

**定义 8.3.1 (跨学科批判)**
跨学科批判是对跨学科理论和方法的批判性分析。

**学科边界批判：**
$$\text{BoundaryCriticism}(I) \iff \text{UnclearBoundaries}(I)$$

**整合批判：**
$$\text{IntegrationCriticism}(I) \iff \text{PoorIntegration}(I)$$

**应用批判：**
$$\text{ApplicationCriticism}(I) \iff \text{InappropriateApplication}(I)$$
